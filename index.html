How to Play Hangman

Learn to Play in Seconds!

It's easy to play! Just follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Category

Select a category that interests you, like Movies, Science, or General Knowledge.

Step 2: Guess the Letters

Start guessing letters. If the letter is in the word, it will appear in the correct position.

Step 3: Save the Hangman

Make sure to guess correctly before the hangman is drawn! You have a limited number of wrong guesses.
